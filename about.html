JUSTIN CHAN'S CREATIVE PORTFOLIO

ABOUT

All of my work is rooted in visual and media design, where attention to clean layouts, expressive color, and balanced composition is key. Often, I look to the density of a place like Hong Kong and the forms that make up its visual rhythm for inspiration, regardless of what piece of graphic design I am working on, such as book cover design or editoral spread design.

PURPOSE

This portfolio is meant to be a reflection of my evolution as a visual designer. It brings together the projects that most closely address my interests of illustration, typography, and editorial design, as well as being a platform for me to continue to develop my aesthetic as a designer. It is my hope that, through this website, the viewer can grasp the ideas behind each piece.
