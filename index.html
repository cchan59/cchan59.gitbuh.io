ABOUT ME

I am a creative media student with a focus on visual and media design. I work primarily with Adobe Illustrator, exploring composition, color, and shape through graphic illustrations and design-centered projects.

I enjoy creating visuals that are simple, expressive, and inspired by the aesthetics and cultural atmosphere of Hong Kong.

My goal is to continue developing my personal design style and to explore how visual media can communicate mood, identity, and storytelling in subtle yet impactful ways.
